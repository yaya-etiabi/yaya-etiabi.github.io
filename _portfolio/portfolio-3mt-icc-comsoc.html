I was a finalist in the Three Minute Thesis (3MT) competition organized by IEEE ComSoc during the 2023 IEEE ICC Conference in Rome, Italy. The competition challenged participants to present their research clearly and concisely in just 3 minutes, aiming to make complex ideas accessible to a wide audience.
