I served as a panelist at an event hosted by APTOM (Association of Professionals of Togo in Morocco). The theme of the discussion was "Artificial Intelligence in the Professional World: Opportunities and Challenges."

My contribution focused on helping participants better understand AI technologies, their applications in various professional contexts, and the opportunities they offer.

The event provided a platform to engage with professionals and share insights that could empower them to navigate the AI hype in the workplace.
