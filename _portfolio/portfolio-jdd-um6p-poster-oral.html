I took part in several editions of the Doctoral Days at UM6P (University Mohammed VI Polytechnique), where I presented multiple posters and gave an oral presentation on my ongoing research (at the time).

One of the highlights of the event was being awarded Best Oral Presenter. The presentation covered my work on machine learning for indoor localization, with an emphasis on federated learning. It was a rewarding experience to share my findings and receive feedback from peers.
