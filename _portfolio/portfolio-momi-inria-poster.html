I participated in the MOMI Event, held at the Inria Center of Université Côte d'Azur, France. This event provided a platform for researchers to present their work and exchange ideas.

My contribution was a poster presentation focused on Federated Learning for Indoor Localization.

During the event, I was awarded Best Poster Presentation. It was an opportunity to receive constructive feedback, discuss research ideas, and engage with other participants.
