I attended the 2023 IEEE VTC (Vehicular Technology Conference) held in Florence, Italy, as a presenting author. I presented my work titled "Deep Reinforcement Learning for LoRa Localization", which explores innovative techniques to improve localization accuracy in low-power wide-area networks like LoRa.

Presenting at this conference allowed me to share my research with a diverse audience of experts and researchers in the field, receive valuable feedback, and engage in discussions that provided new perspectives on my work.
